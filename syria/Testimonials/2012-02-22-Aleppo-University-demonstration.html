Short story of a Demonstration at Aleppo Uni. on Feb. 22th, 2012

From Telecomix agent Muhammad in Aleppo (reachable on IRC) who participated

It took place on 22-2-2012, it was a huge demonstration in the university of Aleppo. We marched the university and we were more than 7000 of demonstrators.
We arrived to the Square of Medicine College (where you see the flags), then raised the flag of independence instead of the current flag.
We attacked a marble picture of Hafez Assad in that square.
Then we got out of the university to the University Square and there we were about 1500 of demonstrators, then the security forces started to shoot us using live bullets, I have had a news that one student was injured, some arrested.
As a result of this great demonstration, the University File was transferred from Military Security to Air-force Intelligence (AlMukhabarat AlJawwieh), AlMukhabarat AlJawwieh is the most criminal, dangerous and the dirtiest intelligence branch in Syria.
